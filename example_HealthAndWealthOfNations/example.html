This is a recreation in D3 of Gapminder’s Wealth & Health of Nations, made famous by Hans Rosling’s memorable 2006 TED talk. It shows the dynamic fluctuation in per-capita income (x), life expectancy (y) and population (area) of 180 nations over the last 209 years. Nations are colored by geographic region; mouseover to read their names.

Adapted from Mike Bostock's adaptation of "The Wealth and Health of Nations": https://bost.ocks.org/mike/nations/

This version has been updated to be compatible with d3 version 4, with extra commenting and a stylistic change or two.
